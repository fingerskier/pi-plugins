# SKiDL Prompt Templates

Use `/skill:skidl` for workflow guidance, then invoke one of these templates:

- `/skidl-amplifier` — Design a non-inverting or inverting op-amp amplifier circuit
- `/skidl-antenna-matching` — Design an impedance matching network for RF antenna
- `/skidl-battery-charger` — Design a Li-ion/LiPo battery charging circuit
- `/skidl-filter` — Design an active low-pass, high-pass, or band-pass filter
- `/skidl-i2c-bus` — Design an I2C bus with pull-ups and multiple device connections
- `/skidl-led-circuit` — Design an LED driver circuit with current limiting
- `/skidl-logic-level-shifter` — Design a voltage level translation circuit
- `/skidl-microcontroller` — Design a microcontroller circuit with essential support components
- `/skidl-motor-driver` — Design an H-bridge or MOSFET motor driver circuit
- `/skidl-oscillator` — Design a 555 timer or crystal oscillator circuit
- `/skidl-power-supply` — Design a linear or switching voltage regulator circuit
- `/skidl-sensor-interface` — Design an analog sensor input with signal conditioning for ADC
- `/skidl-spi-bus` — Design an SPI bus with chip selects for multiple peripherals
- `/skidl-uart-interface` — Design a UART/RS-232 or UART/USB level converter interface
- `/skidl-usb-interface` — Design a USB connector interface with ESD protection
- `/skidl-voltage-divider` — Design a resistive voltage divider circuit with ratio calculation
